BOILED EGGS

1. eggs -take as many as you want. Recommended weekly amount is two, though.
2. pour some water in a pan, it could be tap water.
3. put all your eggs in the pan;)
4. place pan on a ckitchen stove.
5. let the water boil, wait another 5 min.
6. take the pan of the stove, put it under cold tap water, let eggs chill a little.
7. pill eggs.

Bon apetit!)
